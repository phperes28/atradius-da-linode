What is Trade Credit Insurance?

Trade credit insurance is a form of insurance that protects B2B companies who are providing credit when selling to their customers. Therefore if you're providing credit to a business that is liable for the payment, whether locally or internationally, a trade credit insurance policy with Atradius can offer three key benefits

  • Grow your business 2x faster
  • Extend more credit to existing buyers
  • Choosing your buyers wisely

Secure bank funding A trade credit insurance policy can provide the lender with additional comfort on the loan, so you may not have to put up personal assets like your house. Plus, you may also find that the amount you’re able to borrow is actually increased.
